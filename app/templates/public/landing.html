The Canadian Truck Parking Crisis

Canada's trucking industry moves over $900 billion in goods annually, yet finding safe and legal parking remains one of the biggest daily challenges for commercial drivers. With over 300,000 truck drivers on Canadian roads, the shortage of designated truck parking spots has reached critical levels, particularly along major corridors like the Trans-Canada Highway, the 401 in Ontario, and Highway 1 through British Columbia.

Drivers are often forced to park on highway shoulders, entrance ramps, or in unauthorized locations, putting themselves and other road users at risk. Hours-of-service regulations require mandatory rest breaks, but without adequate parking infrastructure, compliance becomes a daily struggle. This disconnect between regulation and reality impacts driver safety, freight efficiency, and Canada's supply chain reliability.

Why Truck Parking Club Exists

Truck Parking Club by TruckerPro was created to bridge the gap between drivers who need parking and property owners who have available space. Our platform brings together truck stops, rest areas, private lots, industrial yards, and travel centres in a single, searchable directory that covers every province and territory in Canada.

Whether you are a long-haul driver planning an overnight stop along the Trans-Canada, a regional carrier looking for monthly parking near a distribution hub, or a property owner with vacant land near a highway interchange, Truck Parking Club connects you with the right solution.

Safe, Secure, and Verified

Every listing on Truck Parking Club includes detailed information about available amenities, security features, LCV (Long Combination Vehicle) capability, and pricing. Our verified badge program ensures that drivers can trust the quality and accuracy of listings. From washroom and shower facilities to Wi-Fi, electrical hookups, and on-site security, our filters help you find exactly what you need.

We are committed to improving the truck parking landscape in Canada, one listing at a time. By making parking easier to find and book, we help drivers stay safe, stay compliant, and keep Canada's supply chain moving.
